Volunteer Opportunities

Choose how you want to help

Food Delivery Driver

Help collect and deliver food donations to those in need. Flexible scheduling available.

  • Valid driver's license
  • 2-4 hours per week
  • Weekend availability

Food Bank Assistant

Sort and organize food at local food banks. Help maintain inventory and assist visitors.

  • No experience needed
  • 3-5 hours per week
  • Weekday shifts

Helpline Support

Answer calls and help coordinate food assistance. Provide information and support.

  • Good communication skills
  • 4-6 hours per week
  • Remote work available

Community Outreach

Help spread awareness about our services. Organize events and recruit volunteers.

  • Public speaking skills
  • 2-3 hours per week
  • Flexible schedule
